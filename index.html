About Chelsea

Creative Director
of Controlled Chaos.

Chelsea Rittenmyer is a storyteller by nature and a seasoned Director of Content & Creative Strategy with over 16 years of experience leading creative campaigns across film, television, and brand media.

Currently at Amazon, she serves as Creative Director and lead behind live-action and educational video content within Amazon Devices and Digital Services — managing multimedia content for Alexa, Tablets, Kindle, and Prime Video across global product launches.

Before Amazon, Chelsea honed her craft at CreativeLive, shaping the look and feel of world-class photo and video education. Her career spans the full production spectrum — from concept and strategy through camera, post, and final delivery.

Based in Seattle, WA. Available worldwide.
